This is the personal and professional website of Aaron B. Wong.

I'm a neuroscientist curious about how the brain works, also in light of how the envirionment and evolution has shaped neural processes. I think we can draw inspiration from artificial neural networks and AI models to understand our own biological neural networks, just as how ANNs were inspired from biology. Here, I share my thoughts on science, art, and life in general.

My own research studies how we sense the world (sensory systems), with a focus on hearing. If you are interested, feel free to visit my [lab website](https://neuro.nl/research/wong).

I have been wondering where to share some of the weird activities I have been doing, which are sometimes but more often not related to work. I figured that a blog might be a good place to document them. 

I am not sure how persistent I will be in creating these posts, so please don't put too much of an expectation on them. &#x1F606;

However, in case I managed, I hope you will find them interesting! &#x1F60A;
